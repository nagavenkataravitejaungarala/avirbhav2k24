About Avirbhav

Avirbhav is our annual fest celebrating innovation, creativity, and talent. With a mix of technical, non-technical, and gaming events, it offers something for everyone:

Technical Events

Engage in coding challenges, hackathons, and tech quizzes to test and showcase your skills.

Non-Technical Events

Explore your artistic side with cultural performances, debates, and creative competitions.

Games and Fun

Enjoy e-sports, traditional games, and fun activities that bring excitement and camaraderie.

Join us at Avirbhav for an unforgettable experience of learning, competition, and fun!
